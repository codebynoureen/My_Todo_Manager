    ℹ️ About This App

    MY Todo Manager is a smart daily task organizer that helps you stay on top of your goals. Add tasks with priorities and due dates, track your progress, and get inspired through the productivity gallery. Weather integration helps you plan outdoor tasks, and your data is always saved locally so nothing gets lost.

    Key Features
    • Add, edit, delete tasks with priority & category
    • Search, filter & sort your task list
    • Real-time statistics & progress tracking
    • Productivity inspiration gallery with slider
    • Live weather lookup by city
    • Dark / light mode toggle
    • Auto-save to browser storage
